How to Play Roblox, Fortnite, and More Using nowgg.fun (Frogies Arcade)

  1. Open Arctic 1.0: Launch the Arctic 1.0 browser on your device.
  2. Visit the Site: In the address bar, enter nowgg.fun and press Enter.
  3. Select Proxy Mode: When asked how you're connecting, choose Proxy.
  4. Enter Your IP Digits:
  • Click Me to view your IPv4 address.
  • It will look similar to 104.57.131.243.
  • Use only the first three digits (example: 104).
  • Enter those digits into the input box and confirm.
  1. Play: You can now access Roblox, Fortnite, and other games through nowgg.fun by Frogies Arcade.
