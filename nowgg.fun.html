How to Play Roblox, Fortnite, and More with Frogies Arcade

  1. Launch Arctic 1.0: Open the Arctic 1.0 browser on your device.
  2. Go to the Website: In the Arctic 1.0 address bar, type nowgg.fun and hit Enter.
  3. Choose Proxy Option: You'll be prompted to select whether you're behind a web proxy or playing directly. Choose the Proxy option.
  4. Enter the Code: When asked for a ip, type 164 into the input box and hit Enter.
  5. Start Playing: You should now be able to play Roblox, Fortnite, and other games through Frogies Arcade
